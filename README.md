🎮 TabletSpoof v4.2 — By Rafael System

Magisk/KSU module that makes your phone identify itself as a Xiaomi Pad 6 Pro to the game. Result: tablet graphics, wider field of view and smoother experience.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
📋 REQUIREMENTS
• Root with Magisk or KernelSU
• Android 10 or higher
• Stock and custom ROMs supported (LineageOS, PixelExperience, crDroid, etc.)

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
⚙️ HOW IT WORKS
The module automatically applies the Xiaomi Pad 6 Pro props when you open the game and reverts them when you close it. No permanent changes to the system.

The toggle and game list are managed from the KernelSU WebUI or directly in Magisk.
